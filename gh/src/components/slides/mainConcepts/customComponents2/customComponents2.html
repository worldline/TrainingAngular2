Main Concepts Custom Components - What do we know (...)

  • Our Components/Directives are parameterizables through the Property Binding mechanism, which is a top to down communication
    • The properties bindings can be declared through the inputs attribute
    • Or with the @Input() annotation
  • With the help of events, you can communicate from a child component to a parent one, which is a down to top communication
    • the event to throw can be declare with the output attribute
    • Or with the @Output() annotation
  • Our Components/Directives could depend on services, and we could want specific providers for them
    • It's what the providers attribute is for
    • Remember that it's available bot at the @Component level and at the @NgModule level
  • We have access to custom methods to make specific treatments at some points
